# AC-2 Account Management

## Control Summary
This control covers how user accounts are created, approved, reviewed, modified, disabled, and removed.

## Implementation Notes
- Define allowed and prohibited account types.
- Assign account owners or managers.
- Require approval before account creation.
- Review accounts on a regular schedule.
- Disable or remove accounts when they are no longer needed.

## Evidence
- Account request or approval record.
- Access review or recertification record.
- Termination or deprovisioning record.
- IAM or system screenshot showing account status.
